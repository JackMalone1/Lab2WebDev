What's good about the game?

It is a completely free to play game. The game also gives you a lot of resources as a free to play player
So you don't have to feel like you have to pay money for the game to get all of the characters.

Why do I like the game?

Since it is a phone game, it is pretty easy to just start playing it whenever.
I also like the game because it allows you to play characters from all of the
Full Final Fantasy games together which I like since I've played a couple
Of these games.
There is also a pretty good community around the game which makes it easy to interact with other people.
