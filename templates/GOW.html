What's good about the game?

The graphics of the game are really good quality
It is also the first God of War game made for the ps4
So there is a big improvement in graphics.
The controls of the game are also really good and the combat and puzzles in
the game are really engaging.

Why do I like the game?

  1. It has really good characters and a good story
  2. the combat is engaging
  3. plenty of puzzles to break up combat
  4. lots of items to collect so you can customise gear
  5. difficult side content gives you something to work towards
  6. longest God of War game and only one made specifically for the ps4

What do others think of the game?


From the reviews of the game that I could find online, both critics and players alike
Seem to really enjoy this game.
I think the main reason for this is the fact that this is the only
God of War game that was made for the current gen of consoles
so people were waiting for a while for a new game.
I think a new setting in Norse mythology also helped the game
To be liked more as it gave it a fresh feeling
As the other games in the series all took place in Greek mythology.
